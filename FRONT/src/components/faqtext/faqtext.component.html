Frequently Asked Questions

What does the chatbot do exactly?

Our AI-powered chatbot helps assess and monitor the risk of heart failure by analyzing user-provided data and offering personalized recommendations. It assists both patients and healthcare providers in early detection and prevention.

How does the AI determine the risk level?

The chatbot uses advanced algorithms to analyze health metrics like age, lifestyle, medical history, and symptoms. It leverages a vast dataset of clinical information to provide accurate risk assessments.

Is my personal data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and comply with GDPR and other data protection standards. All personal data is encrypted and securely stored, ensuring your information remains confidential.

Can healthcare providers use this tool?

Absolutely. The chatbot is designed to assist healthcare professionals by providing risk analysis and facilitating early intervention for at-risk patients.

Is the chatbot medically certified?

While the chatbot is built using medically validated data, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for diagnosis and treatment.

How often is the chatbot updated?

We regularly update the chatbot to ensure it aligns with the latest medical research and guidelines. Updates are automatically integrated for all users.

How can I get started?

Getting started is simple. Sign up on our platform, input your details, and start interacting with the chatbot for personalized health insights.
