Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Heartsense. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our AI-powered chatbot designed to help prevent heart failure risks.

Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and demographic information.
  • Health Data: Information provided through interactions with the chatbot, such as symptoms or health history.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our platform, including session details and preferences.

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve our chatbot services.
  • Analyze health patterns and risks to better personalize recommendations.
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, or support.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or regulation.
  • With trusted partners to provide essential services (e.g., hosting or analytics).
  • With your explicit consent.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
